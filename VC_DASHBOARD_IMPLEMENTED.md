### Tab 1: Overview ⭐⭐⭐
**What's Included**:
- ✅ 4 Large stat cards (Departments, Teachers, Subjects, Timetables)
- ✅ Today's Progress card (shows current slot, completed, remaining)
- ✅ Department Cards Grid (click to view timetable)
  - Shows teachers, subjects, rooms per department
  - Active/Pending status indicator
  - Click to jump to timetable view
- ✅ Live Classes Display (real-time, updates every minute)
  - Shows all classes happening now
  - Color-coded cards
  - Department, teacher, room info
  - Lab badge for lab classes

**Benefits**:
- Quick overview of entire university
- See what's happening right now
- Easy navigation to department details

### Tab 2: University Grid (Heatmap) ⭐⭐⭐
**What's Included**:
- ✅ Complete heatmap grid (Departments × Days × Slots)
- ✅ Color-coded by class density:
  - Gray: Empty (0 classes)
  - Green: Low (1-2 classes)
  - Blue: Medium (3-4 classes)
  - Orange: High (5-6 classes)
  - Red: Very High (7+ classes)
- ✅ Interactive hover tooltips
- ✅ Legend explaining colors
- ✅ Total classes per department shown

**Benefits**:
- Bird's eye view of entire university schedule
- Identify peak hours at a glance
- Spot underutilized time slots
- Compare department workloads visually

### Tab 3: Timetables ⭐⭐⭐
**What's Included**:
- ✅ Department dropdown selector
